About Us

NashDevOps is a group for curious individuals that are interested in collaborating with other like-minded people that have passion for the DevOps Culture.

Sponsored Community

This is a Sponsored community, so that it’s free for the Members to attend. We welcome all Sponsors! They can include, but are not limited to: Technology Vendors, Tech Consulting Firms & Partners, Recruiting Agencies, & Local Businesses which have a Corp or HQ presence in Nashville, looking to attract top talent, by showing that they’re focusing on innovation.

Member Driven

This is a Member Driven community, whereby we rely on the support of our members to organize, contribute, collaborate, and share knowledge and experience. Our Members include a wide range of roles that are involved in/or around Information Technology Organizations. Everyone from the C-Levels and Executive Leadership to Architects, Engineers, Practitioners and Technology Enthusiasts.
