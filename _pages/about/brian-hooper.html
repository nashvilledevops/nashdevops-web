Hello!

My name is Brian Hooper. I am a husband, father, and technologist who lives, works, and plays in Nashville, Tennessee.

I enjoy working with startups and enterprises around the globe supporting the delivery of their continuous improvement efforts through infrastructure as code, advanced platform and automation capabilities. DevOps is the combination of cultural philosophies, practices, and tools that helps teams deliver end-to-end applications and infrastructure solutions safely with velocity.

What I've Done

I am a leader and practitioner who has been working in the field of technology for over 15 years. Presently, I am enjoying my roles as a Founder at Gadgetry.io and Senior DevOps Consultant at Amazon Web Services (AWS) serving customers at large Enterprises as well as lean Startups.

If you want to know more I keep my resume updated on LinkedIn.

A few things that I think are noteworthy:

  • Supported several Nashville Startups with launching in Amazon Web Services (AWS) using Infrastructure as Code
  • Completed several Datacenter Migrations to Amazon Web Services (AWS) as a Managed Code Bases
  • Launched the Edo Interactive Platform as a Managed Service (Edo + Visa Europe)
  • Co-Creator of a Leadership and Communication Workshop Series at Unum
  • Co-Founded NoogaJS JavaScript Community in Chattanooga, Tennessee

These days I am focused on Platform Engineering and Cloud Architecture. I spend majority of my time as a Senior DevOps Consultant at Amazon Web Services (AWS) working with enterprise product teams on their migration to the cloud, sunsetting of legacy datacenters, adoption of infrastructure as code, and building DevOps solutions via AWS services and open source technology.
